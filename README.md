# HR_Attrition_Analysis
HR Analysis: Employee Attrition Analysis
Problem Formulation
Why are workers quitting? This project is an analysis of IBM HR data to find out major patterns and drivers of employee attrition.

Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn and Jupyter notebook

Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition with 1,470 employees, 35 variables

Summary of Key Findings
Overall attrition at 16% – above the industry average
Sales department has maximum attrition rate of 21%
Those that left were paid 40% less than those that stayed
highest attrition is in age group 20-30 years
Median tenure for leavers was 3 years vs 7 years for stayers
Attrition rate doubles with low job satisfaction

Business advice
View Sales salary bands now
Set up 6-monthly reviews of compensation
Build structured onboarding programs for first 3 years
Hold stay interviews with employees aged 20-30 years.
